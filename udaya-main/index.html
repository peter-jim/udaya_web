ABOUT UDAYA

THE MANIFESTO

The conflicts, oppression, and deception we are currently experiencing stem from the traditional order that has severed trust between people. Outdated social rules, cultural identity differences, and language barriers act like a thick wall, separating various groups, organizations, and nations. So we are committed to resolving the trust divide by conducting a "Break the Wall" movement through technology. And eventually enable people to reach a weak consensus model in a trustless environment, explore multi-dimensional organization for productivity, and unleash vast creativity.

Who we are

We are a group of entrepreneurs and investors working in crypto since 2015. We devote ourselves to a positive and wide influence on society by decentralized technology.

Who are we looking for

Social shapers who share our passion for sharing public goods. Engineers who use science and creativity to build a better infrastructure of living. Both long-term thinkers and ambitious doers.

What we can offer

Support could vary based on each individual and team’s different needs, including but not limited to, grant, research, marketing, economic design, etc.
